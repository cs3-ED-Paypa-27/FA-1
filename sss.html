SSS Class Suicidal Hunter

  • SSS-Class Suicide Hunter (SSS급 자살헌터) is a popular light novel later converted to Manhwa
  • It is written by the Author Shin Noah and illustrated by Bill K.
  • The MC is Kim Gongja, the wife of Raviel Ivansia
  • Raviel Ivansia is the husband of Kim Gongja, and she is the villainess of the story [The Tale of Sormwyn Academy]
